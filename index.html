Our Story

Three Generations of Culinary Tradition

TUBATAY FAMILY has been serving authentic traditional cuisine for over three generations. Our recipes have been passed down through our family, preserving the true flavors of our heritage.

We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to bring you an unforgettable dining experience.

"Кип «Тубатай Вкусно»!" - This is not just our motto, it's our promise to you.

1950

Our Humble Beginnings

Grandmother Aisylu started cooking traditional dishes in her small home kitchen, serving neighbors and friends.

1980

First Restaurant

The family opened their first small restaurant in the cultural district, introducing traditional flavors to the city.

2005

Modern Expansion

TUBATAY FAMILY moved to its current location, combining traditional recipes with modern dining experience.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

Now run by the third generation, we continue to honor our traditions while innovating for today's diners.
